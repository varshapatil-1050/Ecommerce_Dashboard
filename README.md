# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard & Performance Tracker

## 📌 Project Overview

In today's competitive e-commerce market, businesses generate large volumes of sales and customer data every day. However, raw data alone cannot help organizations make strategic decisions. This project focuses on transforming transactional sales data into meaningful business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of revenue performance, customer distribution, product category trends, and sales patterns across different countries. By leveraging data visualization techniques, the project enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify profitable product segments, understand customer behavior, and support data-driven decision-making.

This solution helps business managers answer critical questions such as:

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which countries contribute the most sales?
* How are customers distributed across product categories?
* What are the sales trends over time?
* Which customer segments should be targeted for growth

# 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce sales data and create an interactive reporting solution that allows businesses to:

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Track revenue across product categories.
* Understand customer demographics.
* Analyze geographical sales distribution.
* Identify market opportunities and growth trends.
* Support strategic business decisions through visual analytics.

# 📈 Detailed Chart Analysis

## 1. Revenue by Product Category (Horizontal Bar Chart)

### Purpose

This visualization compares revenue generated across different product categories.

### Categories

* Fashion
* Electronics
* Beauty
* Home

### Why This Chart Matters

Businesses need to know which product categories contribute most to revenue.

### Key Findings

* Fashion generates the highest revenue.
* Electronics closely follows Fashion.
* Beauty and Home contribute comparatively lower revenue.

### Business Impact

This insight helps businesses:

* Prioritize high-performing categories.
* Allocate marketing budgets effectively.
* Optimize inventory planning.
* Improve product assortment strategies.


## 2. Product Category Distribution by Gender (Donut Chart)

### Purpose

This chart shows how customers are distributed across product categories.

### Why This Chart Matters

Understanding customer preferences helps businesses create targeted marketing campaigns.

### Key Findings

Customer purchases are fairly balanced across:

* Beauty
* Fashion
* Electronics
* Home

### Business Impact

This information can help:

* Design personalized promotions.
* Improve customer engagement.
* Understand category popularity.
